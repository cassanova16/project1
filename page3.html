In this article, we’ll be talking about The Kardashians Hulu reality TV show, The Kardashian Family, and some of the factors that may have edged it to become Hulu’s biggest series premiere in US history.

The Kardashians is the sequel of Keeping Up with The Kardashians; a 20-season reality TV show that aired from 2007-2021. Just like its predecessor, the Kardashians Hulu stars the Kardashian family as its main cast too. It’s a no-brainer since the show is titled in their name.

Before we discuss the hypothesis of what may have edged The Kardashians’ success, let’s quickly run a breakdown of the show’s concept for new (or potential) viewers.

The Kardashians is a reality TV show centered on America’s most beloved and controversial family, the Kardashian family. While it’s about the entire family, the show revolves around the Kardashian sisters: 44-year-old Kourtney Kardashian, 42-year-old Kim Kardashian, and 38-year-old Khloe Kardashian.

The other cast includes: Rob Kardashian, the Kardashian brother who is named after their father Robert Kardashian who was famous for the OJ Simpsons case, Kris Jenner (their mother), and the Kardashian-Jenner sisters, Kendall Jenner, and Kylie Jenner.

Now, that we’ve covered that, let’s look at some of the reasons that may have edged The Kardashians to become Hulu’s biggest series premiere in US history.

Two years ago, it was announced that Keeping Up with the Kardashians would be ending after its fourteen-year run, and this left loyal fans hungry for more. It’s a no-brainer since the TV show was a thrilling, emotional roller coaster, and comedic show, focused on America’s most beloved and controversial family.

Keeping Up with the Kardashians had a lot of fun moments, ranging from Khloe going to jail to Kim and Khloe throwing fists on screen, and the insight the show gave into what Kim Kardashian and Kanye West’s relationship was like.
